Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [04/17/2024]

Netro Creative ("Netro Creative", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("users", "you", or "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information that we obtain through our mobile application, Geniepers (the "App").

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and IP address. This information is used to provide and improve the App and to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about your use of the App, such as the features you access, the wallpapers you generate and share, and your interactions with other users. This information is used to improve the App and to provide you with a better user experience.
  • Email Address: We may collect your email address for account authentication purposes. This allows you to securely access and manage your Geniepers account.
  • In-App Purchases: We offer in-app purchases of coins, which are used to generate images within the app. When you make an in-app purchase, we may collect your transaction details, such as the amount purchased and date of transaction. We use a secure payment gateway to process all transactions, and we do not store your full credit card information on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Authentication: We use your email address to verify your identity and allow you to log in to your Geniepers account.
  • In-App Purchases: We use transaction details to fulfill your in-app purchases of coins and track your purchase history.

Information Sharing

  • We may share your transaction details with payment processors to complete your in-app purchases. These processors are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

Advertising

We may display in-app advertising to help support the development and maintenance of Geniepers. We work with advertising partners who use non-personally identifiable information to deliver targeted ads. We do not share your personal information with our advertising partners without your consent.

Your Choices

You can choose to opt out of certain types of data collection by adjusting your device settings. You can also delete your account at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with information, please contact us. We will delete any information we have collected from your child.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the App.

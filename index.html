Privacy Policy for Geniepers

At Geniepers, a product of Netro Creative, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our wallpaper application, which now includes targeted ads, in-app purchases, and customer email authentication and payment verification features.

Collection of Your Information

When you use Geniepers, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and payment information when you make in-app purchases or use features that require authentication or payment verification.

  • Targeted Advertising Data: We may collect information about your interests and preferences to provide you with targeted advertisements within the application. This information may include your interactions with advertisements, your browsing history, and demographic information.

Use of Your Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize our services, including targeted advertisements based on your interests and preferences.
  • To process transactions and in-app purchases, including authentication and payment verification.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and providing customer support.
  • To analyze and improve Geniepers, including monitoring usage patterns and identifying areas for enhancement.
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms of service, and protect our rights and interests.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information to third parties for the following purposes:

  • To service providers and vendors who assist us in providing and maintaining our services, including payment processors, advertising partners, and analytics providers.
  • To comply with legal requirements, such as responding to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to protect our rights, property, or safety and the rights, property, or safety of others.

Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law. If you wish to request the deletion of your information, please contact us at support@netrocreative.com.

Your Choices

You may choose to opt-out of targeted advertising within the application settings or by contacting us directly at support@netrocreative.com. However, please note that opting out of targeted advertising does not mean you will no longer receive advertisements; rather, the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@netrocreative.com.
